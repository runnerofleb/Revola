FAQ

Quick answers.

Simple answers before you start.

How much can I request?

Businesses can request funding options up to $2M. Actual availability depends on revenue, time in business, industry, documentation, existing obligations, and funding partner criteria.

Is Revola a bank?

No. Revola Capital is a business funding marketplace and partner network. Funding may be provided through third-party funding partners depending on the transaction and available options.

Does applying guarantee funding?

No. Submitting an inquiry starts a review. Approval, amounts, pricing, terms, and timing depend on your business profile and funding partner criteria.

When does funding make sense?

Funding can make sense when capital helps create revenue, protect revenue, bridge timing, or support a clear business need. It may not make sense if repayment does not fit the business.
