How it works

Simple, organized, and built around speed.

Revola turns a scattered funding process into a clear workflow for merchants and referral partners.

Apply or submit

Merchants apply directly, or a partner submits the business through a tracked referral flow.

Review profile

Business details, revenue, documents, funding needs, and existing positions are organized for review.

Match options

The file is matched with funding partners and options that may fit the business profile.

Fund and track

Merchants move forward with selected offers while partners can follow status and payout progress.

Get started

Check funding options or become a partner.

Use this starter form for your MVP. Later, this can connect to your CRM, email automation, document upload system, and partner portal.
