Why Revola

Funding should not feel like a runaround. So we made the rules plain.

Revola is designed to make the funding process clearer before you commit.

We are not a lender.

Revola is a marketplace. We help organize your profile and route it to funding partners who compete for your business — we never lend to you directly.

You do not pay us.

Revola is paid by funding partners when a match works out, never by you. No application fees, no charge to build or keep your Capital Passport.

No surprise products.

You see the path before you commit, and you choose it. We do not quietly steer you into a product you did not ask for. Your data, your decision.
