FAQ

Questions business owners and partners ask first.

This copy is written to feel trustworthy and conversion-friendly while avoiding overpromising.

How much funding can a business request?

Businesses can request working capital options up to $2M. Actual availability depends on revenue, time in business, industry, documentation, existing obligations, and funding partner criteria.

Is Revola a lender?

Revola is positioned as a business funding marketplace and partner network. Funding may be provided through third-party funding partners, depending on the transaction and available options.

Who can become a referral partner?

CPAs, bookkeepers, consultants, payment processing reps, payroll reps, business brokers, independent contractors, and ISOs may be a fit if they work with business owners who need capital.

What information is usually needed?

Most funding reviews start with basic business information, owner contact details, monthly revenue, time in business, funding amount requested, use of funds, and recent business bank statements when needed.
