FAQ

Funding, explained clearly.

Revola is designed to make business funding easier to understand before you commit to a path.

Is Revola a direct lender?

Revola is designed as a funding marketplace and advisory layer. We help businesses understand possible funding paths and connect with appropriate lending partners.

Will checking eligibility affect credit?

No hard credit pull is required to start the Revola eligibility flow. Specific lender reviews may have their own requirements later in the process.

What products can Revola help compare?

Working capital, business lines of credit, equipment financing, invoice financing, expansion capital, SBA-style paths, purchase-order financing, franchise funding, and debt consolidation options.

Who is the best fit?

Revola is built for businesses across industries that have revenue, a clear use of funds, and a need to compare financing options without chasing multiple lenders.
