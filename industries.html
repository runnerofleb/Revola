FAQ

Industry funding, explained clearly.

Revola is designed to make funding easier to understand by starting with the way your business actually works.

Does industry affect funding options?

Yes. Industry can influence cash-flow patterns, documentation, use of funds, risk profile, and which funding paths may make the most sense.

What if my industry is not listed?

You can still apply. Choose the closest category or select “Other” in the application, and Revola can organize your profile from there.

Can Revola help if my business is seasonal?

Seasonal businesses can still explore options. The key is understanding revenue cycles, timing, funding purpose, and repayment fit.

Do all industries qualify for the same products?

No. Different industries may be better suited for different products such as working capital, equipment financing, invoice financing, lines of credit, or SBA-style paths.
