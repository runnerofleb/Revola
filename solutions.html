FAQ

Funding options, explained clearly.

Revola is designed to make business funding easier to understand before you commit to a path.

How does Revola know which solution fits?

Revola starts with your industry, funding amount, revenue, time in business, credit range, and use of funds. Those signals help organize likely funding paths for review.

Will checking options affect credit?

No hard credit pull is required to start the Revola eligibility flow. Specific lender reviews may have their own requirements later in the process.

Can I compare more than one funding type?

Yes. Many businesses may be a fit for more than one capital path. Revola is designed to help compare tradeoffs such as speed, documentation, structure, and fit.

What if I am not sure what I need?

Choose “Not sure yet” in the application. Revola can use your business profile and funding purpose to help identify possible paths.

Ready to compare funding options?

Start with one simple eligibility flow. Revola helps identify the capital paths that match your business stage, urgency, and growth goals.
