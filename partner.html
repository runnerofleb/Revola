FAQ

Partnerships, explained clearly.

Revola is designed to give partners a clean way to support client funding needs without becoming a lender or broker themselves.

Who can become a Revola partner?

Accountants, consultants, agencies, equipment dealers, franchisors, platforms, associations, lenders, brokers, and other trusted businesses that serve business owners.

Does a partner need lending experience?

No. Many partners simply identify a client need and send the client into Revola’s intake process. Revola handles the funding profile, education, and review workflow.

Can Revola be embedded into another platform?

Yes. The long-term partner model can support embedded funding experiences for SaaS platforms, marketplaces, franchise systems, and business communities.

How are partner economics handled?

Economics depend on partner type, referral volume, funding product, regulatory considerations, and commercial structure. Terms should be reviewed individually.
